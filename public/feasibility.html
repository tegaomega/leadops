Overview

What Is LeadOps?

A plain-English explanation for anyone evaluating this business.

Think of LeadOps as a fishing net for paying customers. Most small businesses in Houston — restaurants, salons, plumbers, food trucks — show up on Google Maps but have no website. They're invisible to anyone searching on a phone. That's your customer. LeadOps finds them automatically, ranks them by likelihood to buy, and gives your sales team one shared place to track every conversation in real time.
🔍
Find
Scans Google Maps across all Houston ZIP codes for businesses with no website
📊
Score
Ranks each business 1–10 based on reviews, phone availability, and operating status
🤝
Close
Gives all 3 team members a shared live board to manage outreach without stepping on each other
