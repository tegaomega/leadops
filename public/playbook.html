The "Lost Opportunity" Call Target: High Priority (7+)

Context: Use when they have 50+ reviews but NO website. You are highlighting that they are already successful, but "leaking" customers to competitors who look more professional online.

"Hi [Name], I'm [Your Name] with [Company]. I'm calling because I was actually just looking for a [Industry] in [City] and found you on Google Maps.

You have incredible reviews—honestly, some of the best I've seen in the area. But I noticed something: when I went to click your website to check your [Services/Menu], there wasn't one listed.

In a market like [City], about 70% of high-end customers skip a business without a site, even with great reviews. I'm building out professional hubs for [Industry] that include [Killer Feature].

Why it works: It starts with a compliment. It uses "negative urgency" (losing customers). It offers a "mockup" (low friction) instead of a "meeting" (high friction).

Objection Killers

The "Feel-Felt-Found" method for turning a NO into a YES.

"I have a Facebook Page already."

Reframing Strategy

"I totally understand—Facebook is great for social updates. However, Facebook is rented land. If their algorithm changes or your account gets flagged, your business vanishes. A website is your digital real estate—you own it, you control it, and most importantly, it's what Google shows first in local searches."

"I'm too busy, I don't need leads."

Reframing Strategy

"That's exactly why we need to talk. My system isn't just about leads—it's about Efficiency. Every hour you spend answering the phone for basic questions about your hours or services is an hour you aren't [Plumbing/Cooking]. A Wix site automates your FAQs and Bookings so you get your time back."

"My nephew can build one for free."

Reframing Strategy

"Free is a great price! But usually, 'free' doesn't come with Ongoing Management. If your site breaks or needs an update on a Friday night, will your nephew be there? I provide professional-grade hosting, security, and 24/7 management so your business stays online without you ever lifting a finger."

"How much does it cost?"

Reframing Strategy

"Great question. Before I give you a number, let me ask—how many new customers do you get per week? Because our Starter plan at $149/month pays for itself if it brings in just one new client. And our Growth plan at $299 typically drives 5-10x ROI in the first 90 days."
